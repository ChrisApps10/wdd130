Lower Kern Family Fun

Perfect for families and first-time rafters, this gentle float on the Lower Kern offers stunning scenery and a relaxing introduction to whitewater rafting. Enjoy calm stretches mixed with beginner-friendly rapids.

  • Duration: Half-day or Full-day
  • Difficulty: Beginner (Class I-II)
  • Price: $35 per person

Upper Kern Thrill Ride

Navigate the splashy and adrenaline-pumping Class III-IV rapids of the Upper Kern River. This trip is ideal for adventure seekers looking for an exciting day on the water.

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Difficulty: Intermediate to Advanced (Class III-IV)
  • Price: $45 per person

Forks of the Kern Expedition

For the ultimate thrill-seekers, this remote and rugged expedition tackles the technical Class V rapids of the Forks of the Kern. Conquer over 40 rapids, some technical, some huge, all unforgettable.

  • Duration: 3 Days
  • Difficulty: Expert (Class V)
  • Price: $170 per person
